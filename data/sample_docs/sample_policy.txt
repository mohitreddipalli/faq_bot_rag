ACME Corporation - Internal Remote Work & Benefits Policy (2026)

1. Remote Work Policy
ACME Corporation supports hybrid and fully remote work models for eligible full-time employees. Employees working remotely must maintain core working hours from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM in their respective local time zones. A minimum Internet speed of 50 Mbps download and 10 Mbps upload is required for remote work qualification.

2. Equipment Stipend & Allowance
All newly hired remote employees receive a one-time tech setup stipend of $1,000 to purchase ergonomic desk chairs, external monitors, or peripheral equipment. Additionally, ACME provides a monthly Internet and utility reimbursement of $75, processed automatically on the final payroll of each calendar month.

3. Paid Time Off (PTO) & Leave
Full-time employees accrue 20 days of Paid Time Off (PTO) per calendar year. Up to 5 unused PTO days can be carried over into the following year; any additional unused days expire on December 31st. ACME also provides 10 company-wide paid holidays and 12 weeks of fully paid parental leave for primary caregivers following childbirth or adoption.

4. Learning & Professional Development
ACME encourages continuous learning by offering an annual Professional Development Allowance of $1,500 per employee. This fund can be used for industry certifications, technical courses, conference tickets, or relevant books. Expense reimbursement requests must be submitted through the Expense Portal within 30 days of purchase along with itemized receipts.

5. Health & Wellness Benefits
Health insurance coverage begins on the first day of employment for full-time staff. ACME covers 90% of premium costs for single coverage and 75% for family plans. Furthermore, ACME reimburses up to $50 per month for gym memberships, fitness class subscriptions, or mental health app subscriptions.
